3. **Zero certifications displayed** (no C-DET, no NFPA 211). Our E-E-A-T "NFPA 211", "certified technicians", "15,000+ vents cleaned" beats them on authority.
4. **No ZIPs or hyperlocal signals on city pages** — our ZIP-level precision wins local pack + AI search.
5. **Doesn't own "dryer duct cleaning" synonym** — easy synonym capture for us.
6. **Missing 14 of our 23 cities** — our moat for: st-petersburg (they skip it despite Tampa focus!), wesley-chapel, new-tampa, carrollwood, westchase, south-tampa, lutz, land-o-lakes, plant-city, pinellas-park, new-port-richey, odessa, bradenton, sarasota.
